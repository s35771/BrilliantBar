Real ingredients

Clean label. Quiet confidence.

Pulsar is built on light-roast almond butter, tahini, coconut oil, resistant tapioca fiber, acacia fiber, defatted almond flour, and sea salt. The formula avoids the loud, candy-like experience of standard protein bars and keeps the texture chewy, satisfying, and easy to eat quickly.

  • Light-roast almond butter, tahini, coconut oil, and sesame oil
  • Tagatose, monk fruit, resistant tapioca fiber syrup, and acacia fiber
  • L-theanine, lion's mane 8:1 extract, citicoline, and phosphatidylserine
  • Sunflower lecithin, sea salt, and a spore-based probiotic
